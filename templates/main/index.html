About

Learn More About AHSS

Empowering Young Scientists Across Ethiopia

We unite science and technology clubs to create a collaborative ecosystem for innovation and learning.

Through shared resources, mentorship, and collaborative projects, we're building the next generation of Ethiopian scientists, engineers, and innovators.

Collaborative Environment

Connect with like-minded students and work together on innovative projects that solve real-world problems.

Skill Development

Gain hands-on experience in robotics, coding, research, and other STEM fields through workshops and projects.
